CAMBADA is the RoboCup Middle-Size League soccer team of the University of Aveiro.

Smart Open Campus envisions the research and development of an ecosystem, centered at the University of Aveiro, where data are automatically gathered and integrated from multiple sources, from personal health to routine daily chores. It represents a practical platform for enabling and testing developments for the smart city and the assisted living society.
